# Context: Comprehensive Admission, Fees, and Scholarship Information for other major Undergraduate Programs (Design, Architecture, Media, Humanities, Basic Sciences) for Bangladeshi Students at Sharda University.

---
## 1. Program Overview & Schools

Sharda University offers a diverse range of undergraduate programs beyond Engineering, Business, and Health Sciences. These programs are housed in specialized schools, each with a focus on creative, scientific, and social disciplines.

### Programs Covered in this Document:

#### Sharda School of Design, Architecture & Planning
- **Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch)**
- **Bachelor of Design (B.Design)**
  - Specializations: Interior Design, Fashion Design, Communication Design
- **Bachelor of Visual Arts (Applied Arts)**

#### Sharda School of Media, Film and Entertainment
- **B.A. - Film Television & OTT Production**
- **B.A. - Journalism & Mass Communication**
- **B.Sc. - Animation, VFX and Gaming Design**
  - Specializations: Animation & VFX, Gaming Design

#### Sharda School of Humanities & Social Sciences
- **B.A. (General)**
  - Subjects: English, History, Sociology, Political Science, Geography, International Relations
- **B.A. - Psychology**
- **B.A. - Economics**

#### Sharda School of Basic Sciences & Research
- **B.Sc. (General)**
  - Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Zoology
- **B.Sc. (Specialized)**
  - Subjects: Computational Physics, Renewable Energy, Computational Chemistry, Environmental Science, Computational Mathematics and Statistics, Data Science and Analytics, Bio-Chemistry, Biotechnology, Microbiology, Food Science & Technology.

## 2. Admission Requirements for Bangladeshi Students

- **Eligibility:** Varies by program.
  - **B.Arch:** Requires a background with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  - **B.Sc. (Science Subjects):** Requires a Science stream background in HSC.
  - **B.A., B.Design, B.A. (Media):** Open to students from all streams (Science, Arts, Commerce).
- **Duration:** Most programs are 3 or 4 years. B.Arch is 5 years.
- **Required Documents:**
  - Class 10 (SSC/Dakhil) Marksheet & Certificate.
  - Class 12 (HSC/Alim) Marksheet & Certificate.
  - Valid Passport (or Birth Certificate/NID for initial application).

## 4. Scholarship Policy for Bangladeshi Students

### Scholarship Slabs (Based on HSC/Diploma CGPA):
- **50% Scholarship (for CGPA 3.5 - 5.0):**
  - B.Arch, B.Design, B.A. (Film & TV), B.A. (Journalism & Mass Comm.) are eligible.
- **20% Scholarship (for CGPA 3.0 and above):**
  - B.A. (General), B.A. (Psychology), B.A. (Economics), B.Sc. (Basic Sciences), B.Sc. (Data Science), and Bachelor of Visual Arts are eligible for a 20% scholarship. These programs are **NOT** on the 50% scholarship list.

### Important Scholarship Conditions:
- **Applicability:** The scholarship is applied ONLY to the **tuition fee**.
- **Continuation Criteria:** To maintain the scholarship in subsequent years, the student must pass all exams without any backlogs or fails and maintain a minimum of 75% attendance.
- **Single Scholarship Rule:** A student can only avail one scholarship per academic year.
