# Context: Comprehensive Admission, Fees, and Scholarship Information for Undergraduate (Hons./Research) Programs for Bangladeshi Students at Sharda University.

---
## 1. Program Overview & Concept

Sharda University offers 4-year (Hons./Research) undergraduate programs as an advanced alternative to the standard 3-year degrees. These programs are aligned with India's New Education Policy (NEP) and are designed for students who wish to pursue deeper research, specialized knowledge, or a direct pathway to a Ph.D.

### Programs Covered in this Document:

#### Sharda School of Media, Film and Entertainment
- **B.A. (Hons./Research) - Film Television & OTT Production**
- **B.A. (Hons./Research) - Journalism & Mass Communication**
- **B.Sc. (Hons./Research) - Animation, VFX and Gaming Design**

#### Sharda School of Humanities & Social Sciences
- **B.A. (Hons./Research) - General** (English, History, Sociology, Political Science, etc.)
- **B.A. (Hons./Research) - Psychology**
- **B.A. (Hons./Research) - Economics**

#### Sharda School of Basic Sciences & Research
- **B.Sc. (Hons./Research) - General** (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Zoology)
- **B.Sc. (Hons./Research) - Specialized** (Environmental Science, Biotechnology, etc.)
- **B.Sc. (Hons./Research) - Data Science and Analytics**

---
## 2. Admission Requirements for Bangladeshi Students

- **Eligibility:** The eligibility criteria are the same as their 3-year counterparts. A relevant background in HSC/12th grade is required (e.g., Science stream for B.Sc. Hons.).
- **Duration:** All Hons./Research programs are **4 years** long.
- **Required Documents:**
  - Class 10 (SSC/Dakhil) Marksheet & Certificate.
  - Class 12 (HSC/Alim) Marksheet & Certificate.
  - Valid Passport (or Birth Certificate/NID for initial application).

## 4. Scholarship Policy for Bangladeshi Students

The scholarship policy for Hons./Research programs is identical to their standard 3-year counterparts.

### Scholarship Slabs (Based on HSC/Diploma CGPA):
- **50% Scholarship (for CGPA 3.5 - 5.0):**
  - B.A. (Hons./Research) in Film & TV and Journalism are eligible.
- **20% Scholarship (for CGPA 3.0 and above):**
  - B.Sc. (Hons./Research) in Animation/VFX, all B.A. (Hons./Research) in Humanities, and all B.Sc. (Hons./Research) in Basic Sciences are eligible for a 20% scholarship.

### Important Scholarship Conditions:
- **Applicability:** The scholarship is applied ONLY to the **tuition fee** for all four years.
- **Continuation Criteria:** To maintain the scholarship throughout the 4-year duration, the student must pass all exams without any backlogs or fails and maintain a minimum of 75% attendance each year.
- **Single Scholarship Rule:** A student can only avail one scholarship per academic year.
