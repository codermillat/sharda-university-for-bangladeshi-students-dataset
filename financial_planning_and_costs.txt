# Financial Planning Guide for Bangladeshi Students (2025-26)

## 1. Total Cost Breakdown (2025-26 Academic Year)

### **Sharda University - Best Value Option:**
- **Tuition Fee:** ₹2,80,000/year → ₹1,40,000 (with 50% scholarship)
- **Hostel + Mess:** ₹1,50,000/year (AC double sharing recommended)
- **One-time fees:** ₹52,000 (admission + security deposit)
- **Total 4-year cost:** ₹12-14 lakh including living expenses

### **Comparative Annual Costs (2025-26):**
- **Galgotias University:** ₹1,89,000 tuition + ₹1,40,000 living = ₹3,29,000/year
- **Amity University:** ₹3,80,000 tuition + ₹1,70,000 living = ₹5,50,000/year
- **NIU:** ₹1,40,000 tuition + ₹1,20,000 living = ₹2,60,000/year

### **Monthly Living Expenses (2025-26):**
- **Food (hostel mess + occasional outside):** ₹4,000-6,000
- **Local transport:** ₹1,500-2,500
- **Mobile/internet:** ₹800-1,200
- **Personal expenses:** ₹3,000-5,000
- **Total monthly budget:** ₹9,000-15,000

## 2. বাংলাদেশ থেকে Fee Payment Process

### **Legal Payment Methods (2025-26):**
- **Student File (Recommended):** University offer letter নিয়ে commercial bank এ যান
  - **Banks:** Eastern Bank, City Bank, Prime Bank, BRAC Bank
  - **Process:** Bank directly transfer করবে university account এ
  - **Cost:** 0.25% service charge
  - **Timeline:** 3-5 working days

- **Wire Transfer (TT):** Parents directly transfer করতে পারেন
- **Flywire/Payment Gateway:** Amity University credit card payment accept করে

### **What NOT to Use:**
- **bKash/Nagad:** Not allowed for international education payments
- **Hundi/Informal channels:** Illegal and risky
- **Cash through agents:** Avoid large cash transactions

### **Currency Notes:**
- **Fees quoted:** INR (₹) তে
- **Payment processing:** Bank handles currency conversion automatically
- **Fixed rate:** University provides exact amount in BDT equivalent

## 3. Visa এর জন্য Financial Documentation (2025-26)

### **Mandatory Requirements:**
- **Bank Statement:** Minimum **BDT 1,00,000** balance required
  - **Purpose:** Living expenses proof (tuition fees আলাদা)
  - **Duration:** Last 6 months statement
  - **Account:** Parent/guardian এর account acceptable

- **Affidavit of Support:** বাবা-মা signed statement
  - **Content:** "আমি সব educational expenses bear করবো"
  - **Notarized:** Lawyer/notary public থেকে certified

### **Family Budget Planning:**
**For middle-class families (monthly income ₹50,000-1,00,000 BDT):**
- **Total investment:** ₹12-14 lakh over 4 years
- **Annual burden:** ₹3-3.5 lakh per year
- **Monthly saving needed:** ৳25,000-30,000 BDT
- **ROI timeline:** 18-20 months after graduation

### **Scholarship Impact on Family Budget:**
- **Without scholarship:** ₹18-20 lakh total cost
- **With 50% scholarship:** ₹12-14 lakh total cost
- **Family savings:** ₹6-8 lakh over 4 years

**Conclusion:** Sharda University scholarship makes quality education affordable for middle-class Bangladeshi families।

—source: Indian High Commission Dhaka, University fee structures 2025-26
